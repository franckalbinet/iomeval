

# Objective 1

## Title: Collect and utilize accurate and disaggregated data as a basis for evidence-based policies

## Associated actions

- 1.  Elaborate and implement a comprehensive strategy for improving
      migration data at local, national, regional and global levels
- 2.  Improve international comparability and compatibility of migration
      statistics and national data systems
- 3.  Develop a global programme to build and enhance national
      capacities in data collection, analysis and dissemination
- 4.  Collect, analyse and use data on the effects and benefits of
      migration
- 5.  Support further development of and collaboration between existing
      global and regional databases and depositories
- 6.  Establish and strengthen regional centres for research and
      training on migration or migration observatories
- 7.  Improve national data collection by integrating migration-related
      topics in national censuses, as early as practicable
- 8.  Conduct household, labour force and other surveys to collect
      information on the social and economic integration of migrants
- 1.  Enhance collaboration between State units responsible for
      migration data and national statistical offices
- 10. Develop and use country-specific migration profiles
- 11. Cooperate with relevant stakeholders in countries of origin,
      transit and destination

# Objective 2

## Title: Minimize the adverse drivers and structural factors that compel people to leave their country of origin

## Associated actions

- 1.  Promote the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable
      Development
- 2.  Invest in programmes that accelerate States’ fulfilment of the
      Sustainable Development Goals
- 3.  Establish or strengthen mechanisms to monitor and anticipate the
      development of risks and threats
- 4.  Invest in sustainable development at local and national levels in
      all regions
- 5.  Invest in human capital development
- 6.  Strengthen collaboration between humanitarian and development
      actors
- 7.  Account for migrants in national emergency preparedness and
      response
- 8.  Strengthen joint analysis and sharing of information to better
      map, understand, predict and address migration movements
- 1.  Develop adaptation and resilience strategies to sudden-onset and
      slow-onset natural disasters
- 10. Integrate displacement considerations into disaster preparedness
      strategies and promote cooperation with neighbouring and other
      relevant countries
- 11. Harmonize and develop approaches and mechanisms at subregional and
      regional levels
- 12. Develop coherent approaches to address the challenges of migration
      movements in the context of sudden-onset and slow-onset natural
      disasters

# Objective 3

## Title: Provide accurate and timely information at all stages of migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Launch and publicize a centralized and publicly accessible
      national website
- 2.  Promote and improve systematic bilateral, regional and
      international cooperation and dialogue
- 3.  Establish open and accessible information points along relevant
      migration routes
- 4.  Provide newly arrived migrants with targeted, gender-responsive,
      child-sensitive, accessible and comprehensive information and
      legal guidance
- 5.  Promote multi-lingual, gender-responsive and evidence-based
      information campaigns

# Objective 4

## Title: Ensure that all migrants have proof of legal identity and adequate documentation

## Associated actions

- 1.  Improve civil registry systems
- 2.  Harmonize travel documents in line with the specifications of the
      International Civil Aviation Organization
- 3.  Ensure adequate, timely, reliable and accessible consular
      documentation to our nationals residing in other countries
- 4.  Facilitate access to personal documentation
- 5.  Strengthen measures to reduce statelessness
- 6.  Review and revise requirements to prove nationality at service
      delivery centres
- 7.  Build upon existing practices at the local level that facilitate
      participation in community life

# Objective 5

## Title: Enhance availability and flexibility of pathways for regular migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Develop human rights-based and gender-responsive bilateral,
      regional and multilateral labour mobility agreements
- 2.  Facilitate regional and cross-regional labour mobility through
      international and bilateral cooperation arrangements
- 3.  Review and revise existing options and pathways for regular
      migration
- 4.  Develop flexible, rights-based and gender-responsive labour
      mobility schemes for migrants
- 5.  Promote effective skills matching in the national economy by
      involving local authorities and other relevant stakeholders
- 6.  Foster efficient and effective skills-matching programmes
- 7.  Develop or build on existing national and regional practices for
      admission and stay of appropriate duration
- 8.  Cooperate to identify, develop and strengthen solutions for
      migrants compelled to leave their countries of origin
- 1.  Facilitate access to procedures for family reunification for
      migrants
- 10. Expand available options for academic mobility

# Objective 6

## Title: Facilitate Fair And Ethical Recruitment And Safeguard Conditions That Ensure Decent Work

## Associated actions

- 1.  Promote signature, ratification, accession and implementation of
      relevant international instruments
- 2.  Build upon the work of existing bilateral, subregional and
      regional platforms
- 3.  Improve regulations on public and private recruitment agencies
- 4.  Establish partnerships with all relevant stakeholders
- 5.  Enact and implement national laws that sanction human and labour
      rights violations
- 6.  Strengthen the enforcement of fair and ethical recruitment and
      decent work norms and policies
- 7.  Develop and strengthen labour migration and fair and ethical
      recruitment processes
- 8.  Take measures that prohibit the confiscation or non-consensual
      retention of work contracts
- 1.  Provide migrant workers engaged in remunerated and contractual
      labour with the same labour rights and protections
- 10. Ensure migrants working in the informal economy have safe access
      to effective reporting, complaint, and redress mechanisms
- 11. Review relevant national labour laws, employment policies and
      programmes
- 12. Develop and improve national policies and programmes relating to
      international labour mobility

# Objective 7

## Title: Address and Reduce Vulnerabilities in Migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Review relevant policies and practices to ensure they do not
      create, exacerbate or unintentionally increase vulnerabilities of
      migrants
- 2.  Establish comprehensive policies and develop partnerships
- 3.  Develop gender-responsive migration policies
- 4.  Review relevant existing labour laws and work conditions
- 5.  Account for migrant children in national child protection systems
- 6.  Protect unaccompanied and separated children at all stages of
      migration
- 7.  Ensure migrants have access to public or affordable independent
      legal assistance and representation
- 8.  Develop accessible and expedient procedures that facilitate
      transitions from one status to another
- 1.  Build on existing practices to facilitate access for migrants in
      an irregular status
- 10. Apply specific support measures
- 11. Involve local authorities and relevant stakeholders in the
      identification, referral and assistance of migrants
- 12. Develop national policies and programmes to improve national
      responses

# Objective 8

## Title: Save Lives and Establish Coordinated International Efforts on Missing Migrants

## Associated actions

- 1.  Develop procedures and agreements on search and rescue of migrants
- 2.  Review the impacts of migration-related policies and laws to
      ensure that these do not raise or create the risk of migrants
      going missing
- 3.  Enable migrants to communicate with their families without delay
      to inform them that they are alive
- 4.  Establish transnational coordination channels
- 5.  Collect, centralize and systematize data regarding corpses and
      ensure traceability after burial
- 6.  Make all efforts, including through international cooperation, to
      recover, identify and repatriate the remains of deceased migrants

# Objective 9

## Title: Strengthen the transnational response to smuggling of migrants

## Associated actions

- 1.  Promote ratification, accession and implementation of the Protocol
      against the Smuggling of Migrants by Land, Sea and Air
- 2.  Use transnational, regional and bilateral mechanisms to share
      relevant information and intelligence on smuggling routes
- 3.  Develop gender-responsive and child-sensitive cooperation
      protocols along migration routes
- 4.  Adopt legislative and other measures as may be necessary to
      establish the smuggling of migrants as a criminal offence
- 5.  Design, review or amend relevant policies and procedures to
      distinguish between the crimes of smuggling of migrants and
      trafficking in persons
- 6.  Take measures to prevent the smuggling of migrants along the
      migration cycle in partnership with other States and relevant
      stakeholders

# Objective 10

## Title: Prevent, Combat And Eradicate Trafficking In Persons In The Context Of International Migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Promote, ratification, accession and implementation of the
      Protocol to Prevent, Suppress and Punish Trafficking in Persons
      Especially Women and Children
- 2.  Promote the implementation of the Global Plan of Action to Combat
      Trafficking in Persons and take into consideration relevant
      recommendations
- 3.  Monitor irregular migration routes which may be exploited by human
      trafficking networks
- 4.  Share relevant information and intelligence through transnational
      and regional mechanisms
- 5.  Apply measures that address the particular vulnerabilities of
      women, men, girls and boys
- 6.  Ensure that definitions of trafficking in persons used in
      legislation, migration policy and planning are in accordance with
      international law
- 7.  Strengthen legislation and relevant procedures to enhance
      prosecution of traffickers, avoid criminalization of migrants
- 8.  Provide migrants that have become victims of trafficking in
      persons with protection and assistance
- 1.  Create national and local information systems and training
      programmes
- 10. Invest in awareness-raising campaigns

# Objective 13

## Title: Use Migration Detention Only As A Measure Of Last Resort And Work Towards Alternatives

## Associated actions

- 1.  Use existing relevant human rights mechanisms to improve
      independent monitoring of migrant detention
- 2.  Consolidate a comprehensive repository to disseminate best
      practices of human rights based alternatives to detention
- 3.  Review and revise relevant legislation, policies and practices
      related to migration detention
- 4.  Provide access to justice for all migrants in countries of transit
      and destination that are or may be subject to detention
- 5.  Ensure that all migrants in detention are informed about the
      reasons for their detention
- 6.  Reduce the negative and potentially lasting effects of detention
      on migrants
- 7.  Ensure that all governmental authorities and private actors duly
      charged with administering immigration detention do so in a way
      consistent with human rights
- 8.  Protect and respect the rights and best interests of the child at
      all times

# Objective 14

## Title: Enhance Consular Protection, Assistance And Cooperation Throughout The Migration Cycle

## Associated actions

- 1.  Cooperate to build consular capacities
- 2.  Involve relevant consular and immigration personnel in existing
      global and regional fora on migration
- 3.  Conclude bilateral or regional agreements on consular assistance
      and representation
- 4.  Strengthen consular capacities in order to identify, protect and
      assist our nationals abroad who are in a situation of
      vulnerability
- 5.  Provide our nationals abroad the opportunity to register with the
      country of origin
- 6.  Provide consular support to our nationals through advice

# Objective 15

## Title: Provide access to basic services for migrants

## Associated actions

- 1.  Enact laws and take measures to ensure that service delivery does
      not amount to discrimination against migrants
- 2.  Ensure that cooperation between service providers and immigration
      authorities does not exacerbate vulnerabilities of irregular
      migrants
- 3.  Establish and strengthen holistic and easily accessible service
      points at local level
- 4.  Establish or mandate independent institutions at the national or
      local level
- 5.  Incorporate the health needs of migrants in national and local
      health care policies and plans
- 6.  Provide inclusive and equitable quality education to migrant
      children and youth

# Objective 16

## Title: Empower Migrants And Societies To Realize Full Inclusion And Social Cohesion

## Associated actions

- 1.  Promote mutual respect for the cultures, traditions and customs of
      communities of destination and of migrants
- 2.  Establish comprehensive and needs-based pre-departure and
      post-arrival programmes
- 3.  Develop national short, medium and long term policy goals
      regarding the inclusion of migrants in societies
- 4.  Work towards inclusive labour markets and full participation of
      migrant workers in the formal economy
- 5.  Empower migrant women by eliminating gender-based discriminatory
      restrictions
- 6.  Establish community centres or programmes at the local level to
      facilitate migrant participation in the receiving society
- 7.  Capitalize on the skills, cultural and language proficiency of
      migrants and receiving communities
- 8.  Support multicultural activities through sports, music, arts,
      culinary festivals, volunteering and other social events
- 1.  Promote school environments that are welcoming and safe, and
      support the aspirations of migrant children

# Objective 17

## Title: Eliminate All Forms Of Discrimination And Promote Evidence-Based Public Discourse To Shape Perceptions Of Migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Enact, implement or maintain legislation that penalizes hate
      crimes and aggravated hate crimes targeting migrants
- 2.  Empower migrants and communities to denounce any acts of
      incitement to violence directed towards migrants
- 3.  Promote independent, objective and quality reporting of media
      outlets
- 4.  Establish mechanisms to prevent, detect and respond to racial,
      ethnic and religious profiling of migrants
- 5.  Provide migrants, especially migrant women, with access to
      national and regional complaint and redress mechanisms
- 6.  Promote awareness-raising campaigns targeted at communities of
      origin, transit and destination
- 7.  Engage migrants, political, religious and community leaders, as
      well as educators and service providers

## Associated actions

- 1.  Develop standards and guidelines for the mutual recognition of

- 5.  Build global skills partnerships amongst countries
- 6.  Promote inter-institutional networks and collaborative programmes

      institutions
- 7.  Engage in bilateral partnerships and programmes in cooperation
      with relevant stakeholders
- 8.  Cooperate with the private sector and employers
- 1.  Enhance the ability of migrant workers to transition from a job or
      employer to another
- 10. Develop and promote innovative ways to mutually recognize and
      assess formally and informally acquired skills
- 11. Establish screening mechanisms of credentials and offer
      information to migrants
- 12. Cooperate to promote documentation and information tools

# Objective 19

## Associated actions

- 2.  Integrate migration into development planning and sectoral

- 3.  Invest in research on the impact of non-financial contributions of
      migrants and diasporas to sustainable development
- 4.  Facilitate the contributions of migrants and diasporas to their
      countries of origin
- 5.  Develop targeted support programmes and financial products
- 6.  Provide easily accessible information and guidance
- 7.  Enable political participation and engagement of migrants in their
      countries of origin
- 8.  Promote migration policies that optimize the benefits of diasporas
      for countries of origin and destination
- 1.  Cooperate with other States, the private sector and employers
      organizations to enable migrants and diasporas
- 10. Build partnerships between local authorities, local communities,
      the private sector, diasporas, hometown associations and migrant
      organizations

# Objective 21

## Title: Cooperate In Facilitating Safe And Dignified Return And Readmission, As Well As Sustainable Reintegration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Develop and implement bilateral, regional and multilateral
      cooperation frameworks and agreements
- 2.  Promote gender-responsive and child-sensitive return and
      reintegration programmes
- 3.  Cooperate on identification of nationals and issuance of travel
      documents for safe and dignified return and readmission
- 4.  Foster institutional contacts between consular authorities and
      relevant officials from countries of origin and destination
- 5.  Ensure that the return of migrants who do not have the legal right
      to stay on another State’s territory is safe and dignified
- 6.  Establish or strengthen national monitoring mechanisms on return
- 7.  Ensure that return and readmission processes involving children
      are carried out only after a determination of the best interests
      of the child
- 8.  Facilitate the sustainable reintegration of returning migrants
      into community life
- 1.  Identify and address the needs of the communities to which
      migrants return

# Objective 23

## Title: Strengthen International Cooperation And Global Partnerships For Safe, Orderly And Regular Migration

## Associated actions

- 1.  Support other States as we collectively implement the Global
      Compact
- 2.  Increase international and regional cooperation to accelerate the
      implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development
- 3.  Involve and support local authorities in the identification of
      needs and opportunities for international cooperation for the
      effective implementation of the Global Compact
- 4.  Make use of the capacity-building mechanism and build upon other
      existing instruments to strengthen the capacities of relevant
      authorities
- 5.  Conclude bilateral, regional or multilateral mutually beneficial,
      tailored and transparent partnerships
