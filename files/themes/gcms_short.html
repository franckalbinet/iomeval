    Objective 1

    Title: Collect and utilize accurate and disaggregated data as a basis for evidence-based policies

    Associated actions

      1. Elaborate and implement a comprehensive strategy for improving migration data at local, national, regional and global levels
      1. Improve international comparability and compatibility of migration statistics and national data systems
      1. Develop a global programme to build and enhance national capacities in data collection, analysis and dissemination
      1. Collect, analyse and use data on the effects and benefits of migration
      1. Support further development of and collaboration between existing global and regional databases and depositories
      1. Establish and strengthen regional centres for research and training on migration or migration observatories
      1. Improve national data collection by integrating migration-related topics in national censuses, as early as practicable
      1. Conduct household, labour force and other surveys to collect information on the social and economic integration of migrants
      1. Enhance collaboration between State units responsible for migration data and national statistical offices
      1. Develop and use country-specific migration profiles
      1. Cooperate with relevant stakeholders in countries of origin, transit and destination

    Objective 2

    Title: Minimize the adverse drivers and structural factors that compel people to leave their country of origin

    Associated actions

      1. Promote the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development
      1. Invest in programmes that accelerate States’ fulfilment of the Sustainable Development Goals
      1. Establish or strengthen mechanisms to monitor and anticipate the development of risks and threats
      1. Invest in sustainable development at local and national levels in all regions
      1. Invest in human capital development
      1. Strengthen collaboration between humanitarian and development actors
      1. Account for migrants in national emergency preparedness and response
      1. Strengthen joint analysis and sharing of information to better map, understand, predict and address migration movements
      1. Develop adaptation and resilience strategies to sudden-onset and slow-onset natural disasters
      1. Integrate displacement considerations into disaster preparedness strategies and promote cooperation with neighbouring and other relevant countries
      1. Harmonize and develop approaches and mechanisms at subregional and regional levels
      1. Develop coherent approaches to address the challenges of migration movements in the context of sudden-onset and slow-onset natural disasters

    Objective 3

    Title: Provide accurate and timely information at all stages of migration

    Associated actions

      1. Launch and publicize a centralized and publicly accessible national website
      1. Promote and improve systematic bilateral, regional and international cooperation and dialogue
      1. Establish open and accessible information points along relevant migration routes
      1. Provide newly arrived migrants with targeted, gender-responsive, child-sensitive, accessible and comprehensive information and legal guidance
      1. Promote multi-lingual, gender-responsive and evidence-based information campaigns

    Objective 4

    Title: Ensure that all migrants have proof of legal identity and adequate documentation

    Associated actions

      1. Improve civil registry systems
      1. Harmonize travel documents in line with the specifications of the International Civil Aviation Organization
      1. Ensure adequate, timely, reliable and accessible consular documentation to our nationals residing in other countries
      1. Facilitate access to personal documentation
      1. Strengthen measures to reduce statelessness
      1. Review and revise requirements to prove nationality at service delivery centres
      1. Build upon existing practices at the local level that facilitate participation in community life

    Objective 5

    Title: Enhance availability and flexibility of pathways for regular migration

    Associated actions

      1. Develop human rights-based and gender-responsive bilateral, regional and multilateral labour mobility agreements
      1. Facilitate regional and cross-regional labour mobility through international and bilateral cooperation arrangements
      1. Review and revise existing options and pathways for regular migration
      1. Develop flexible, rights-based and gender-responsive labour mobility schemes for migrants
      1. Promote effective skills matching in the national economy by involving local authorities and other relevant stakeholders
      1. Foster efficient and effective skills-matching programmes
      1. Develop or build on existing national and regional practices for admission and stay of appropriate duration
      1. Cooperate to identify, develop and strengthen solutions for migrants compelled to leave their countries of origin
      1. Facilitate access to procedures for family reunification for migrants
      1. Expand available options for academic mobility

    Objective 6

    Title: Facilitate Fair And Ethical Recruitment And Safeguard Conditions That Ensure Decent Work

    Associated actions

      1. Promote signature, ratification, accession and implementation of relevant international instruments
      1. Build upon the work of existing bilateral, subregional and regional platforms
      1. Improve regulations on public and private recruitment agencies
      1. Establish partnerships with all relevant stakeholders
      1. Enact and implement national laws that sanction human and labour rights violations
      1. Strengthen the enforcement of fair and ethical recruitment and decent work norms and policies
      1. Develop and strengthen labour migration and fair and ethical recruitment processes
      1. Take measures that prohibit the confiscation or non-consensual retention of work contracts
      1. Provide migrant workers engaged in remunerated and contractual labour with the same labour rights and protections
      1. Ensure migrants working in the informal economy have safe access to effective reporting, complaint, and redress mechanisms
      1. Review relevant national labour laws, employment policies and programmes
      1. Develop and improve national policies and programmes relating to international labour mobility

    Objective 7

    Title: Address and Reduce Vulnerabilities in Migration

    Associated actions

      1. Review relevant policies and practices to ensure they do not create, exacerbate or unintentionally increase vulnerabilities of migrants
      1. Establish comprehensive policies and develop partnerships
      1. Develop gender-responsive migration policies
      1. Review relevant existing labour laws and work conditions
      1. Account for migrant children in national child protection systems
      1. Protect unaccompanied and separated children at all stages of migration
      1. Ensure migrants have access to public or affordable independent legal assistance and representation
      1. Develop accessible and expedient procedures that facilitate transitions from one status to another
      1. Build on existing practices to facilitate access for migrants in an irregular status
      1. Apply specific support measures
      1. Involve local authorities and relevant stakeholders in the identification, referral and assistance of migrants
      1. Develop national policies and programmes to improve national responses

    Objective 8

    Title: Save Lives and Establish Coordinated International Efforts on Missing Migrants

    Associated actions

      1. Develop procedures and agreements on search and rescue of migrants
      1. Review the impacts of migration-related policies and laws to ensure that these do not raise or create the risk of migrants going missing
      1. Enable migrants to communicate with their families without delay to inform them that they are alive
      1. Establish transnational coordination channels
      1. Collect, centralize and systematize data regarding corpses and ensure traceability after burial
      1. Make all efforts, including through international cooperation, to recover, identify and repatriate the remains of deceased migrants

    Objective 9

    Title: Strengthen the transnational response to smuggling of migrants

    Associated actions

      1. Promote ratification, accession and implementation of the Protocol against the Smuggling of Migrants by Land, Sea and Air
      1. Use transnational, regional and bilateral mechanisms to share relevant information and intelligence on smuggling routes
      1. Develop gender-responsive and child-sensitive cooperation protocols along migration routes
      1. Adopt legislative and other measures as may be necessary to establish the smuggling of migrants as a criminal offence
      1. Design, review or amend relevant policies and procedures to distinguish between the crimes of smuggling of migrants and trafficking in persons
      1. Take measures to prevent the smuggling of migrants along the migration cycle in partnership with other States and relevant stakeholders

    Objective 10

    Title: Prevent, Combat And Eradicate Trafficking In Persons In The Context Of International Migration

    Associated actions

      1. Promote, ratification, accession and implementation of the Protocol to Prevent, Suppress and Punish Trafficking in Persons Especially Women and Children
      1. Promote the implementation of the Global Plan of Action to Combat Trafficking in Persons and take into consideration relevant recommendations
      1. Monitor irregular migration routes which may be exploited by human trafficking networks
      1. Share relevant information and intelligence through transnational and regional mechanisms
      1. Apply measures that address the particular vulnerabilities of women, men, girls and boys
      1. Ensure that definitions of trafficking in persons used in legislation, migration policy and planning are in accordance with international law
      1. Strengthen legislation and relevant procedures to enhance prosecution of traffickers, avoid criminalization of migrants
      1. Provide migrants that have become victims of trafficking in persons with protection and assistance
      1. Create national and local information systems and training programmes
      1. Invest in awareness-raising campaigns

    Objective 11

    Title: Manage Borders In An Integrated, Secure And Coordinated Manner

    Associated actions

      1. Enhance international, regional and cross-regional border management cooperation
      1. Establish appropriate structures and mechanisms for effective integrated border management
      1. Review and revise relevant national procedures for border screening, individual assessment and interview processes
      1. Develop technical cooperation agreements
      1. Ensure that child protection authorities are promptly informed and assigned to participate in procedures
      1. Review and revise relevant laws and regulations
      1. Improve cross-border collaboration among neighbouring and other States

    Objective 12

    Title: Strengthen Certainty And Predictability In Migration Procedures For Appropriate Screening, Assessment And Referral

    Associated actions

      1. Increase transparency and accessibility of migration procedures
      1. Develop and conduct intra- and cross-regional specialized human rights and trauma informed trainings
      1. Establish gender-responsive and child-sensitive referral mechanisms
      1. Ensure that migrant children are promptly identified at places of first arrival in countries of transit and destination
      1. Ensure that, in the context of mixed movements, relevant information on rights and obligations

    Objective 13

    Title: Use Migration Detention Only As A Measure Of Last Resort And Work Towards Alternatives

    Associated actions

      1. Use existing relevant human rights mechanisms to improve independent monitoring of migrant detention
      1. Consolidate a comprehensive repository to disseminate best practices of human rights based alternatives to detention
      1. Review and revise relevant legislation, policies and practices related to migration detention
      1. Provide access to justice for all migrants in countries of transit and destination that are or may be subject to detention
      1. Ensure that all migrants in detention are informed about the reasons for their detention
      1. Reduce the negative and potentially lasting effects of detention on migrants
      1. Ensure that all governmental authorities and private actors duly charged with administering immigration detention do so in a way consistent with human rights
      1. Protect and respect the rights and best interests of the child at all times

    Objective 14

    Title: Enhance Consular Protection, Assistance And Cooperation Throughout The Migration Cycle

    Associated actions

      1. Cooperate to build consular capacities
      1. Involve relevant consular and immigration personnel in existing global and regional fora on migration
      1. Conclude bilateral or regional agreements on consular assistance and representation
      1. Strengthen consular capacities in order to identify, protect and assist our nationals abroad who are in a situation of vulnerability
      1. Provide our nationals abroad the opportunity to register with the country of origin
      1. Provide consular support to our nationals through advice

    Objective 15

    Title: Provide access to basic services for migrants

    Associated actions

      1. Enact laws and take measures to ensure that service delivery does not amount to discrimination against migrants
      1. Ensure that cooperation between service providers and immigration authorities does not exacerbate vulnerabilities of irregular migrants
      1. Establish and strengthen holistic and easily accessible service points at local level
      1. Establish or mandate independent institutions at the national or local level
      1. Incorporate the health needs of migrants in national and local health care policies and plans
      1. Provide inclusive and equitable quality education to migrant children and youth

    Objective 16

    Title: Empower Migrants And Societies To Realize Full Inclusion And Social Cohesion

    Associated actions

      1. Promote mutual respect for the cultures, traditions and customs of communities of destination and of migrants
      1. Establish comprehensive and needs-based pre-departure and post-arrival programmes
      1. Develop national short, medium and long term policy goals regarding the inclusion of migrants in societies
      1. Work towards inclusive labour markets and full participation of migrant workers in the formal economy
      1. Empower migrant women by eliminating gender-based discriminatory restrictions
      1. Establish community centres or programmes at the local level to facilitate migrant participation in the receiving society
      1. Capitalize on the skills, cultural and language proficiency of migrants and receiving communities
      1. Support multicultural activities through sports, music, arts, culinary festivals, volunteering and other social events
      1. Promote school environments that are welcoming and safe, and support the aspirations of migrant children

    Objective 17

    Title: Eliminate All Forms Of Discrimination And Promote Evidence-Based Public Discourse To Shape Perceptions Of Migration

    Associated actions

      1. Enact, implement or maintain legislation that penalizes hate crimes and aggravated hate crimes targeting migrants
      1. Empower migrants and communities to denounce any acts of incitement to violence directed towards migrants
      1. Promote independent, objective and quality reporting of media outlets
      1. Establish mechanisms to prevent, detect and respond to racial, ethnic and religious profiling of migrants
      1. Provide migrants, especially migrant women, with access to national and regional complaint and redress mechanisms
      1. Promote awareness-raising campaigns targeted at communities of origin, transit and destination
      1. Engage migrants, political, religious and community leaders, as well as educators and service providers

    Objective 18

    Title: Invest In Skills Development And Facilitate Mutual Recognition Of Skills, Qualifications And Competences

    Associated actions

      1. Develop standards and guidelines for the mutual recognition of foreign qualifications and non-formally acquired skills
      1. Promote transparency of certifications and compatibility of National Qualifications Frameworks
      1. Conclude bilateral, regional or multilateral mutual recognition agreements
      1. Use technology and digitalization to evaluate and mutually recognize skills more comprehensively
      1. Build global skills partnerships amongst countries
      1. Promote inter-institutional networks and collaborative programmes for partnerships between the private sector and educational institutions
      1. Engage in bilateral partnerships and programmes in cooperation with relevant stakeholders
      1. Cooperate with the private sector and employers
      1. Enhance the ability of migrant workers to transition from a job or employer to another
      1. Develop and promote innovative ways to mutually recognize and assess formally and informally acquired skills
      1. Establish screening mechanisms of credentials and offer information to migrants
      1. Cooperate to promote documentation and information tools

    Objective 19

    Title: Create Conditions For Migrants And Diasporas To Fully Contribute To Sustainable Development In All Countries

    Associated actions

      1. Ensure the full and effective implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and the Addis Ababa Action Agenda
      1. Integrate migration into development planning and sectoral policies at local, national, regional and global levels
      1. Invest in research on the impact of non-financial contributions of migrants and diasporas to sustainable development
      1. Facilitate the contributions of migrants and diasporas to their countries of origin
      1. Develop targeted support programmes and financial products
      1. Provide easily accessible information and guidance
      1. Enable political participation and engagement of migrants in their countries of origin
      1. Promote migration policies that optimize the benefits of diasporas for countries of origin and destination
      1. Cooperate with other States, the private sector and employers organizations to enable migrants and diasporas
      1. Build partnerships between local authorities, local communities, the private sector, diasporas, hometown associations and migrant organizations

    Title: Minimize the adverse drivers and structural factors that compel people to leave their country of origin

    Associated actions

    Objective 21

    Title: Cooperate In Facilitating Safe And Dignified Return And Readmission, As Well As Sustainable Reintegration

    Associated actions

      1. Develop and implement bilateral, regional and multilateral cooperation frameworks and agreements
      1. Promote gender-responsive and child-sensitive return and reintegration programmes
      1. Cooperate on identification of nationals and issuance of travel documents for safe and dignified return and readmission
      1. Foster institutional contacts between consular authorities and relevant officials from countries of origin and destination
      1. Ensure that the return of migrants who do not have the legal right to stay on another State’s territory is safe and dignified
      1. Establish or strengthen national monitoring mechanisms on return
      1. Ensure that return and readmission processes involving children are carried out only after a determination of the best interests of the child
      1. Facilitate the sustainable reintegration of returning migrants into community life
      1. Identify and address the needs of the communities to which migrants return

    Objective 22

    Title: Establish Mechanisms For The Portability Of Social Security Entitlements And Earned Benefits

    Associated actions

      1. Establish or maintain non-discriminatory national social protection systems
      1. Conclude reciprocal bilateral, regional or multilateral social security agreements
      1. Integrate provisions on the portability of entitlements and earned benefits into national social security frameworks

    Objective 23

    Title: Strengthen International Cooperation And Global Partnerships For Safe, Orderly And Regular Migration

    Associated actions

      1. Support other States as we collectively implement the Global Compact
      1. Increase international and regional cooperation to accelerate the implementation of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development
      1. Involve and support local authorities in the identification of needs and opportunities for international cooperation for the effective implementation of the Global Compact
      1. Make use of the capacity-building mechanism and build upon other existing instruments to strengthen the capacities of relevant authorities
      1. Conclude bilateral, regional or multilateral mutually beneficial, tailored and transparent partnerships
