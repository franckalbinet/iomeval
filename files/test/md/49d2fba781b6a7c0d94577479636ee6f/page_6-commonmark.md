Source: IOM Reintegration Handbook As demonstrated in the figure above,
the integrated approach targets three societal levels <sup>6</sup> :

- Individual level: Initiatives to address the specific needs and
  vulnerabilities of returnees and returning family members.
  Reintegration support is provided to individual returnees, based on an
  assessment of their needs.
- Community level: Initiatives that respond to the needs,
  vulnerabilities, and concerns of communities to which migrants return,
  including returnee families and the non-migrant population.
- Structural/system level: Initiatives that promote good governance of
  migration through engagement with local and national authorities and
  stakeholders and supports continuity of assistance through adequate
  local public services.
